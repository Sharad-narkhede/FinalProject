What makes this contest unique?

Vibe is a design-forward coding challenge. We celebrate playful interaction, delightful UX, and accessible builds just as much as technical depth. Whether you're composing generative visuals, crafting ultra-usable tools, or remixing data into art, this is your stage.

  • 48 hours to ship a polished, original project
  • Judged on vibe, craft, accessibility, and originality
  • Solo or team of up to 4

Rules

  • Original work created during the contest window
  • Open-source dependencies are allowed with proper licenses
  • Be kind, inclusive, and follow the Code of Conduct
  • Ensure accessibility: keyboard nav, contrast, and readable content

FAQ

Can I join solo or as a team?

Yes. Teams up to 4, or go solo. You choose your vibe.

Do I keep my IP?

Yes. You own what you build. You grant us permission to showcase it.

What can I submit?

A live demo link and a short write-up with screenshots or video. Source code is optional.
